Projects

Underdog Databases

As a former field organizer, I appreciate voter outreach is critical but hard work. Every bit to optimize volunteers' and field staffers' time goes a long way. To create efficient lists of voter to contact, organizers need access to voter records and an interface to enter voters' reponses. Frustated by how closed off much of the existing software was, I decided to create my own platform.

Underdog Databases is the realization of my hopes for a political data management platform open to small groups and campaigns. With some help from friends, I launched a beta version this winter. Currently, Underdog Databases includes features to build lists of voters based on certain demographics, import voters' responses to questions, and cut turf for canvassing. If you are interested in using Underdog Databases, please contact me at underdogDatabases@gmail.com!

Social Media Investigations

When Gyphon Strategies started its new Data Mining and Analytics team, I came in as a contractor. I developed code to obtain and aggregate data from social media using official APIs. My scripts gave investigators a quick and painless way to find pertinent information on subjects and topics of interest.
