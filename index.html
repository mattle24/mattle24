Projects

Underdog Databases

As a former field organizer, I appreciate voter outreach is critical but hard work. Every bit to optimize volunteers' and field staffers' time goes a long way. To create efficient lists of voter to contact, organizers need access to voter records and an interface to enter voters' reponses. Frustated by how closed off much of the existing software was, I decided to create my own platform.

Underdog Databases was the realization of my hopes for a political data management platform open to small groups and campaigns. Although it is no longer hosted, the Underdog Databases codebase includes features to build lists of voters, track responses, and cut turf for canvassing. If you are interested in using Underdog Databases, please contact me at underdogDatabases@gmail.com!
